Analog CCTV cameras: These cameras use an analog signal to transmit images. They are the most common type of CCTV camera, and they are typically cheaper than other types of cameras. However, they require more power and they are not as easy to install as other types of cameras.

Digital CCTV cameras: These cameras use a digital signal to transmit images. They are more expensive than analog CCTV cameras, but they are easier to install and they use less power.

Benefits of CCTV Service in Australia

Installing a CCTV system is one of the most effective ways to improve the security of your home or business. A CCTV system can deter crime, help to solve crimes that have already been committed and provide valuable evidence in the event of a break-in or other incident.

1. Deterring crime

CCTV systems are a proven deterrent to crime. If potential criminals can see that your property is being monitored, they are less likely to target it. This is especially effective in public places such as car parks, where criminals are more likely to be caught on camera.

2. Helping to solve crimes

If a crime is committed on your property, the footage from your CCTV system could be vital in helping the police to catch the culprit. Clear footage can identify suspects and help to build a case against them.

3. Providing evidence

In the event of a break-in or other incident, the footage from your CCTV system can be used as evidence. This can be helpful in insurance claims and in court proceedings.

4. Peace of mind

Knowing that your property is being monitored can give you peace of mind. You’ll know that you’re deterring criminals and that you have evidence if something does happen.

5. Monitoring employees

If you run a business, you may want to use CCTV to monitor your employees. This can help to improve productivity and to prevent theft.

6. Remote monitoring

Some CCTV systems allow you to view the footage remotely, either on a computer or on a mobile device. This can be useful if you want to check on your property while you’re away.
